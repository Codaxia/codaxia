How Codaxia can help you

Web and application development, technical direction and "Team as a Service"

  • We are here to help you grow your team:
  • - Implementation of a management methodology and a technical infrastructure adapted to the needs of your company
  • - Provision or recruitment of the necessary resources depending on the organization
  • - Follow-up during the growth of the company with a technical management and team lead service
  • Above all, we focus on the quality and solidity of the organization and the code
  • - Cascade or agile organization depending on the project
  • - Training and follow-up of managers and leads already present in your organization
  • - Implementation of a technical stack according to the complexity of the projects (Docker architecture, Git flow, Unit tests and so on)
  • Our expertise is mainly focused on 2 branches: SaaS and cms
  • We mainly work with high-level web languages and frameworks (php, laravel, Javascript, React.js, vue.js)
  • For the infrastructure and the reliability of the code, we work with technologies that allow rigorous monitoring (Docker, git Flow, jest.js, PhpUnit)
  • For the cms part we mainly use wordpress

We recruit our resources according to the chosen organization

- Recruitment is done according to the needs of the project or with the team already in place to respect consistency as much as possible

- We are recruiting English-speaking resources who already have experience in Canadian or American agencies
